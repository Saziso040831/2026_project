This service is exclusively for current students and staff of Durban University of Technology (DUT). You must use your official DUT4Life email address to register.

3. Accurate Information

You agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information when reporting lost or found items. False reports may result in account suspension or disciplinary action.

4. Item Claims

When claiming an item, you must provide truthful information. False claims are considered fraudulent and may lead to:

  • Potential disciplinary action by the university

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Your DUT email address and student number
  • Your full name and contact details
  • Information about lost or found items you report
  • Images of items you upload
  • Claims and interactions on the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Facilitate matching lost and found items
  • Communicate with you about your reports and claims
  • Verify your student status
  • Improve our services

3. Information Sharing

We only share your contact information with other users when:

  • A claim on an item you reported is approved
  • You approve a claim on an item you found
  • Required by law or university policy

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure database storage. However, no system is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Resolved items are moved to history but remain in our system for record-keeping.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies are used.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact the DUT IT Support at itsupport@dut.ac.za
